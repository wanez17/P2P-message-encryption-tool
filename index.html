How To Use

  • Enter your message in the first space provided
  • Click on the ENCRYPT button (red button) to encrypt the message
  • Click on the Copy button below the displayed output to copy the encrypted text.
  • The receiver will also paste the encrypted text in the space provided and click on the DECRYPT button (green button) to reveal the actual text
  • You can also click on the RESET button to reset to clear recent texts
